# Technical Content Operations

Technical social content should be optimized per platform without losing source fidelity.
WeChat benefits from structured long-form Markdown. X works best as a short thread with a
clear opening and practical ending. Xiaohongshu needs concise note formatting, title variants,
and cover image prompts.

The safest workflow is generate, review, validate, then publish. Scheduled publishing should
only pick up content that was explicitly approved by a human.
